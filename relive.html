Case Study

Relive

An AR-powered mobile concept that makes local history more interactive through location-aware storytelling and immersive on-site experiences.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

Dubstech Protothon 2024

Team

4-person interdisciplinary team

ReLive
ReLive

Overview

Relive explores how augmented reality can support learning outside the classroom. The concept overlays historical stories, media, and location-based prompts onto real environments so users can discover history while physically moving through a site.

Problem

Traditional historical content is often static and disconnected from the real places where events happened, making it harder for users to stay engaged.

Solution

We designed a mobile AR experience with guided location triggers, contextual visuals, and narrative checkpoints that turn passive reading into active exploration.

My Contributions

User flow design, interaction patterns, screen layout decisions, visual system direction, and prototype refinement for the core experience.

Design Focus

Accessibility, clarity of navigation, and reducing cognitive load while users are moving in a real-world environment.

Key Experience Flow

  1. Users arrive at a mapped historical site and launch the experience.
  2. Location-aware prompts reveal AR stories, media, and contextual facts.
  3. Users progress through a guided route with checkpoints and reflection moments.
  4. A summary screen helps them review and retain the historical narrative.
